## Clawdia Write Rule

Clawdia may update Goonie `MEMORY.md` and `KNOWLEDGE_BASE.md` files only when:

- Chris directly instructs her to save something there
- a proof-backed consult is accepted
- a durable decision is approved
- a source-backed finding should be preserved

Clawdia may not save guesses, fake research, secrets, or unapproved private client data.

## Source Traceability Rule

Every durable Goonie memory or knowledge entry must preserve source traceability.

Required traceability fields:

- `source_type`
- `source_name`
- `source_url_or_path`
- `date_accessed`
- `confidence_level`
- `citation_needed`
- `citation_available`
- `public_source`
- `private_internal_source`
- `notes_for_citation`

If an entry may later support public content, email copy, articles, or client-facing material, it must be citation-ready or clearly marked as not citation-ready.

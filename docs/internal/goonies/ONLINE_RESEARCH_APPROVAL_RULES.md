# ONLINE_RESEARCH_APPROVAL_RULES
- version: 1.0
- status: active
- last_updated: 2026-04-28

## Rule

The Goonies may research public sources only when Clawdia consults them.

## They Must Research When

- confidence is low
- facts are unclear
- sources conflict
- current information matters
- technical docs may have changed
- laws, rules, or platform behavior may have changed
- the answer could cause damage if wrong

## They May Not

- browse randomly without a consult
- scrape private data
- bypass paywalls
- collect unnecessary personal data
- expose secrets
- use private customer systems unless Chris explicitly approves internal admin use

## If Public Research Is Not Enough

- say public sources are insufficient
- mark confidence LOW
- state what access is missing
- do not guess

- version: 1.0
- status: active
- last_updated: 2026-04-28

## Confidence Levels

### HIGH
- strong sources
- low risk
- clearly in lane

### MEDIUM
- decent sources
- some judgment needed
- safe recommendation possible

### LOW
- unclear sources
- conflicting evidence
- outside lane
- risky or incomplete

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to Chris when:

- business risk is meaningful
- public research is insufficient
- advice could affect spending
- a public claim could be risky if wrong
- private data access would be required
- multiple Goonies disagree and Clawdia cannot resolve it safely

## Clawdia Rule

Clawdia may use Goonie advice to decide next steps, but LOW-confidence advice must be flagged and not treated as settled truth.

# CHUNK KNOWLEDGE BASE
## Purpose

Store Chunk's verified, source-backed research findings for all NexTeam clients and internal projects.

This is not a dump of links.

This is a curated library of traceable truth that Clawdia can trust.

## Where This Goonie Gathers Knowledge

- official docs
- government or regulatory sources
- manufacturer docs
- public records
- public case studies
- trade publications
- approved internal research files

## Client and Jurisdiction Rules

- every durable entry must be tagged to the correct client or internal project
- client facts must not bleed across clients
- every jurisdiction-specific entry must clearly name the state, county, city, or agency when it matters
- Chunk must not assume one state's rules apply to another

## Aquatrace Coverage Scope

Chunk supports Aquatrace research across:

- Florida
- Georgia
- South Carolina
- North Carolina
- Virginia
- Tennessee

Aquatrace research may be narrowed to one market only when the task specifically asks for that location.

## Accepted source types

- official docs
- government or regulatory sources
- manufacturer docs
- public records
- public case studies
- reputable trade sources
- approved internal research files

## Rejected source types

- private email
- private Dropbox
- private CompanyCam
- private Jobber or customer records
- `.env`, tokens, credentials
- login-only sources without approval
- unsupported anonymous claims treated as proof

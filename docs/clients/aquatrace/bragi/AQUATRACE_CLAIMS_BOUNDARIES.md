# AQUATRACE CLAIMS BOUNDARIES

## Never Claim Without Proof

- Do not guarantee every leak can be found in every condition.
- Do not invent pricing.
- Do not invent legal or compliance claims.
- Do not overstate VGB or commercial compliance language.
- Do not imply a waterfall or sheer descent can always be pressure tested in the same way as standard plumbing.
- Do not expose private customer information.

## Safe Positioning

- Aquatrace specializes in swimming pool leak detection and documentation.
- Aquatrace uses diagnostic methods such as pressure testing, dye testing, electronic leak detection, underwater inspection, and documentation.
- Aquatrace helps residential and commercial pool owners understand likely leak sources and next steps.

## Human Review Triggers

- Legal or compliance wording
- Pricing or guarantee wording
- Customer-identifying case-study details
- Any image where content or context is uncertain

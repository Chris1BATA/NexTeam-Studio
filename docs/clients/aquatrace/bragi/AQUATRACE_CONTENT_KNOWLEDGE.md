# AQUATRACE CONTENT KNOWLEDGE

## Positioning

- Aquatrace Swimming Pool Leak Detection is a specialist leak detection company.
- Aquatrace is not a general pool service company.
- Aquatrace performs diagnostics, pressure testing, dye testing, electronic basin scanning, underwater or SCUBA inspection, acoustic listening, and documentation.
- Aquatrace serves residential and commercial pools.
- Aquatrace content should build authority around swimming pool leak detection.
- Aquatrace should be positioned as the specialist referred for swimming pool leak detection.

## Service Experience

- Bucket test guidance
- Pressure testing for plumbing lines
- Dye testing around fittings, skimmers, and penetrations
- Electronic leak detection and basin scanning where appropriate
- Underwater inspection for structural and fitting leak points
- Leak documentation for residential and commercial properties

## Service Boundaries

- Aquatrace detects and documents leak sources.
- Repair guidance can be discussed, but content must not imply Aquatrace performs every repair type unless verified for that case.
- Waterfall and sheer descent pressure-testing limitations require careful wording.
- VGB or main-drain documentation language must stay factual and careful.

## Audience Priorities

- Homeowners who are not sure whether they are dealing with evaporation or a leak
- Commercial pool operators who need documentation and clear next steps
- HOA and property managers who need specialist diagnosis without guesswork

## Voice

- Casual, friendly, informative
- Specific, plain-English, trustworthy
- Never salesy, goofy, or overblown
- Every article should help the reader understand the problem and feel confident calling Aquatrace

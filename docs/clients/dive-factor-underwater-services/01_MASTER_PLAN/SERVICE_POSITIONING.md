# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Service Positioning

## Positioning purpose

This file defines how Dive Factor should be positioned internally during phase-one planning.

It is not approved public copy.

## Brand separation

- Dive Factor = underwater services for boats, docks, lifts, marinas, lake homes, and selected coastal or waterfront assets
- Aquatrace = swimming pool leak detection
- Do not blend Aquatrace messaging, case studies, or public claims into Dive Factor without Chris approval

## Core positioning angle

Dive Factor should be positioned around below-waterline visibility, documentation, and practical inspection support.

Best-fit positioning themes:

- visual proof before expensive guessing
- practical underwater eyes on boats and waterfront assets
- documented condition summaries for owners, managers, and partners
- peace of mind below the waterline

## Best first offer positioning

Anchor offer:

- Dive Factor Below-Waterline Inspection

Primary use cases:

- boat hull and running gear visual check
- dock and lift visual check
- swim-area hazard scan
- post-storm underwater documentation
- pre-purchase visual documentation

## Phase-one buyer focus

### B2C

- lakefront homeowners
- second-home owners
- family boaters
- pontoon, wake boat, and fishing boat owners
- vacation-rental property owners or managers

### B2B

- marinas
- boat rental companies
- boat clubs
- dock builders
- waterfront real estate and vacation-rental partners

## Positioning guardrails

- stay visual and documentation-focused
- do not imply structural certification
- do not imply insurance acceptance
- do not imply OSHA or commercial diving compliance
- do not imply repair, salvage, or emergency-response capability unless verified

## Current recommendation

Lead with inspection and documentation first.

Hold maintenance-heavy, certification-sensitive, and coastal-operations-heavy positioning until compliance and operational review are completed.

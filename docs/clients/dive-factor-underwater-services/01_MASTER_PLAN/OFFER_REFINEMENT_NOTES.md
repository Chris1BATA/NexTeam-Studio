# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Offer Refinement Notes

## What market research supports

- Hartwell supports broad boating and marina demand
- Keowee supports premium waterfront-owner and documentation-heavy positioning
- both markets support a below-waterline inspection anchor offer before broader maintenance positioning

## Recommended first offers

1. Dive Factor Below-Waterline Inspection
2. Dock and Lift Visual Inspection
3. Prop / Running Gear Visual Check
4. Swim Area Hazard Scan

## Recommended phase-two offers

- Lake Homeowner Waterfront Watch Plan
- Marina Partner Plan
- Rental Fleet / Boat Club Plan
- Lost Item Recovery as a secondary add-on or situational offer

## Offer strategy notes

- keep the core offer visual and documentation-led
- use premium-reporting upsells in Keowee
- keep recurring plans gated until operating workflow is clearer

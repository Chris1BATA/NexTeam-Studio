# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Market Memo - Coastal South Carolina

## Sourced findings

- South Carolina's Clean Marina program lists certified coastal marinas, showing an active marina ecosystem with environmental sensitivity.
- Beaufort Downtown Marina is publicly listed with slips, fuel, and pump-out infrastructure.
- Dataw Island Marina markets a working boatyard and DIY maintenance environment.

## Internal interpretation

- Coastal South Carolina has real marina opportunity, but the operational bar is higher than inland lake work.
- This market is attractive for later-stage partner work and documentation services, but hull-cleaning and technical maintenance claims require stronger review.

# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Market Memo - Lake Hartwell

## Question

Does Lake Hartwell show enough boat, marina, dock, and waterfront-property activity to justify phase-one focus for Dive Factor?

## Sourced findings

- Hartwell is one of the Southeast's largest and most visited Corps lakes, with millions of annual visitors, multiple parks, marinas, and campgrounds according to the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers.
- South Carolina DNR lists Hartwell at 56,000 surface acres, 962 miles of shoreline, 45 boat ramps, and 5 marinas with pumpouts.
- USACE publishes a marina list for Hartwell, confirming an established commercial marina footprint.
- Portman Marina positions itself as South Carolina's largest inland marina and states it has approximately 500 slips.
- Big Water Marina markets fuel, rentals, wet slips, dry storage, and campground activity.
- Harbor Light Marina markets rentals and slip rentals on the Georgia/South Carolina border.
- Sun Life Hartwell Marina markets wet slips, indoor boat storage, pontoon rentals, and waterfront amenities.

## Internal interpretation

- Hartwell has the scale and boating density to support an inspection-led service focused on boats, docks, lifts, and lakefront properties.
- The combination of marinas, rentals, slip storage, and waterfront dining suggests active vessel traffic and recurring below-waterline visibility needs.
- The cross-state geography may widen the addressable B2B base, but it also increases travel-zone and service-area planning complexity.

## Dive Factor implications

- Hartwell should remain a phase-one launch market.
- B2B priority targets should include large marinas, rentals, boat clubs, dock builders, and lakefront real estate partners.
- B2C messaging can lean into lake-home, family boating, and second-home peace-of-mind use cases.

## Assumptions

- Assumption: high shoreline and marina activity likely correlates with recurring dock, lift, prop, and hull inspection demand.
- Assumption: premium or multi-boat households around major marinas may respond well to bundled inspection offers.

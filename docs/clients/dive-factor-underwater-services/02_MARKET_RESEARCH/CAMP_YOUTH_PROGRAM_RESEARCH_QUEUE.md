### A. NAUI intro scuba and youth-program standards

Status: in progress

Questions:
- Which exact NAUI program should anchor a camp pool experience?
- What are the minimum ages for confined-water and optional open-water use?
- What parent or guardian signatures are mandatory?
- What medical screening and release forms are mandatory?
- What participant-to-instructor ratios apply?
- What confined-water depth and standing-water limits apply?
- Can a Divemaster run any part of the program without an instructor present?
- What youth-count limits apply in controlled or open water?

Verified sources:
- NAUI Intro to Scuba page:
  - https://www.naui.org/learn/supervised-diver/introduction-to-scuba-introductory-experienceiso-11121/
- NAUI Standards and Policies Manual, January 2024 edition:
  - https://resources.naui.org/media/1580/2024-s-and-p-v10.pdf
- NAUI Young Aquatic Explorers:
  - https://www.naui.org/learn/recognition/young-aquatic-explorers/
- NAUI Skin Diver:
  - https://www.naui.org/learn/apnea/skin-diver/
- NAUI Learn page noting Skin Diving Instructor snorkeling authority:
  - https://www.naui.org/learn/

Verified notes:
- NAUI Introduction to Scuba minimum age is 8 in confined water and 10 in open water.
- Minor in-water instruction requires signed parent/legal guardian release documentation.
- Minor medical forms also require parent/legal guardian signatures.
- Intro scuba ratios are condition-sensitive and must be treated as maximums, not operating targets.

### B. DAN safety training offer fit

Status: partially verified

Questions:
- Which DAN course or course bundle best fits camp staff?
- Can Chris deliver these directly under his current credentials, or is an instructor partnership needed?
- Which course is best for lifeguards, waterfront staff, and trip leaders?

Verified sources:
- DAN Training overview:
  - https://dan.org/education-events/training/
- DAN instructor-led course page:
  - https://dan.org/education-events/instructor-led-courses/

Verified notes:
- DAN BLS covers CPR, AED, foreign body airway obstruction, bleeding management, splinting, burns, medical emergencies, and shock response.
- DAN EO2 is framed for scuba diving and submersion injuries and requires current DAN BLS or another nationally recognized full CPR training prerequisite.
- DAN DFA Pro includes BLS and EO2 elements plus neurological assessment and hazardous marine life injury material.
- DAN states DFA Pro has been accepted by the American Camp Association for staff CPR and first aid requirements.

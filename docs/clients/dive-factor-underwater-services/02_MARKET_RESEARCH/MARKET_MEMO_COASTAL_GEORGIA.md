# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Market Memo - Coastal Georgia

## Sourced findings

- Georgia DNR's Clean Marina program confirms a formal marina stewardship environment in coastal Georgia.
- Jekyll Harbor Marina positions itself as a premier coastal Georgia marina.
- Brunswick Landing Marina markets bottom-cleaning and haul/lift-related services.
- Public marketplace listings show established underwater-service competitors in Savannah.

## Internal interpretation

- Coastal Georgia is commercially interesting, but it is the clearest example of a market that needs compliance, insurance, and scope review before public offer packaging.
- Competition and technical-service expectations appear stronger than in phase-one lake markets.

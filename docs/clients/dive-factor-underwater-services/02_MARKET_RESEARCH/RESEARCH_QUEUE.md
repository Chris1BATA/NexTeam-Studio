# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Research Queue

## Research standard

Every completed research item must include:

- source name
- source URL
- date reviewed
- market or jurisdiction tag
- confidence note
- clear separation between sourced fact and internal assumption

No item is complete without source URLs.

Preferred source mix:

- official tourism or destination sources
- official lake, county, state, or federal sources
- operator websites for marinas, rentals, brokers, and service competitors
- source-dated local business directories only when primary sites are unavailable

Avoid relying on:

- unsourced AI summaries
- forum hearsay
- unverifiable social posts used as standalone proof

# Dive Factor Underwater Services - B2C Demographic Strategy

## Purpose

This file defines the phase-one B2C audience strategy for planning.

## Priority demographics

### Tier 1

- lakefront homeowners around Hartwell
- lakefront homeowners around Keowee
- second-home owners
- premium waterfront owners
- family boat owners

### Tier 2

- pontoon owners
- wake boat owners
- fishing boat owners
- Airbnb and VRBO lake-house owners
- vacation-property caretakers or managers

### Tier 3

- coastal boat or yacht owners after separate review

## Likely triggers

- storm aftermath
- seasonal reopening
- unusual boat performance concern
- visible dock or lift concern
- pre-purchase caution
- missing item recovery need
- swim-area safety concern
- second-home owner wanting documentation without being onsite

## Messaging direction

- peace of mind below the waterline
- visual proof before guessing
- practical underwater documentation for boats and waterfront property
- local help for conditions owners cannot easily see themselves

## Guardrails

- no structural-certification claims
- no insurance-acceptance claims
- no Aquatrace leak-detection positioning
- no repair promises unless verified

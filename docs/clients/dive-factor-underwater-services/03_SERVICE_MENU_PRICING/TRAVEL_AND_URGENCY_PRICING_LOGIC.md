# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Travel and Urgency Pricing Logic

## Travel logic

- zone 1: core local market
- zone 2: extended launch-market travel
- zone 3: expansion-market travel

Travel pricing should scale with:

- drive time
- launch logistics
- setup complexity
- return trip or repeat visit requirement

## Urgency logic

- standard: normal scheduling window
- priority: faster scheduling within normal business cadence
- rush: same-day, next-day, or storm-response style turnaround when operationally possible

## Rule

Travel and urgency should be explicit add-ons later, not hidden inside a vague flat fee.

# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Subscription / RMR Models

## Purpose

Explore recurring-revenue ideas internally without approving them for sale.

## Draft recurring models

### Waterfront Watch Lite

- target buyer: second-home owner
- cadence: seasonal or quarterly
- focus: dock, lift, swim area, and basic condition reporting

### Waterfront Watch Plus

- target buyer: premium homeowner
- cadence: monthly or seasonal plus storm-trigger option
- focus: fuller photo/video documentation and faster reporting

### Marina Partner Plan

- target buyer: marina
- cadence: custom recurring service windows
- focus: customer-referral checks or marina asset checks

### Fleet Visibility Plan

- target buyer: rental fleet or boat club
- cadence: periodic fleet review
- focus: running-gear and issue documentation

## Warning

Recurring commitments should remain internal-only until operations, access rules, and liability posture are reviewed.

# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Recommended Phase One Service Menu

## Purpose

This is the recommended internal phase-one service menu after audit.

## Recommended phase-one launch services

### Primary offer

- Dive Factor Below-Waterline Inspection

### Recommended supporting offers

- Dock and Lift Visual Inspection
- Prop / Running Gear Visual Check
- Swim Area Hazard Scan

## Secondary / situational offer

- Lost Item Recovery

Reason:

- useful and marketable in simple conditions
- but easier to overextend into riskier recovery expectations if promoted too aggressively

## Hold for staged review

- Lake Homeowner Waterfront Watch Plan
- Marina Partner Plan
- Rental Fleet / Boat Club Plan

## Hold for compliance or operational review

- coastal hull cleaning
- anode/zinc service
- heavy rope or entanglement removal
- structural or certified inspection language
- commercial diving contractor positioning
- salvage or emergency positioning

## Recommended pricing posture

- keep price anchors internal-only
- quote from scope, access, travel, urgency, and documentation depth

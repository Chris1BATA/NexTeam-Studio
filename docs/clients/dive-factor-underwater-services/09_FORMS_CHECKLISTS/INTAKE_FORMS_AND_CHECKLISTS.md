# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Intake Forms and Checklists

## Intake forms needed

1. B2C inspection request form
2. B2B partner inquiry form
3. Pre-job site and access form
4. Media/report delivery checklist
5. Post-job proof checklist

## B2C inspection request fields

- name
- phone
- email
- service area
- property or marina name
- asset type
- asset size or approximate scope
- reason for request
- urgency
- preferred timing
- photo or video upload if available
- permission/contact notes
- whether the request is boat, dock, lift, swim area, storm, or mixed scope
- whether written report, fast turnaround, or referral guidance is expected
- acknowledgement that scope is visual/documentation-based unless otherwise agreed

## B2B partner inquiry fields

- business name
- category
- website
- market
- contact name
- contact role
- phone
- email
- fleet, dock, or property count
- recurring need description
- preferred follow-up timing
- geography served
- whether COI, vendor packet, or marina approval steps are required

## Pre-job checklist topics

- authorization confirmed
- location confirmed
- scope confirmed
- hazards discussed
- weather and water conditions checked
- access instructions confirmed
- reporting expectations confirmed
- exclusions acknowledged
- go/no-go safety screen completed
- access-control or marina rules confirmed
- documentation level confirmed

## Post-job proof checklist

- photo and video captured
- files organized
- summary written
- limitations stated
- next action stated
- proof log entry created

## Standard intake outputs to build later

- inspection request form copy
- admin intake checklist
- quote worksheet
- on-site checklist
- report-delivery checklist

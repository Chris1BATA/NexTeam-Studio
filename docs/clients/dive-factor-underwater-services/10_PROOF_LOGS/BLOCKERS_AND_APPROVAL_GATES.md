# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Blockers and Approval Gates

## Active approval gates

- any public publishing
- any external contact
- any website or domain action
- any spend
- any final public pricing sheet
- any legal or compliance conclusion
- any move of a high-risk service from `hold for review` to `ready`

## Current blockers

### Compliance-sensitive service blocker

The following cannot move into approved public offers without additional review:

- OSHA/commercial diving claims
- structural or certified inspection claims
- insurance-approved contractor claims
- coastal hull cleaning
- anode or zinc service
- heavy entanglement removal
- salvage or emergency positioning

### Verification blocker

If market data, contact details, or operational claims cannot be verified from public sources, they should remain blank, qualified, or labeled as assumptions.

## Current status

- no blocker for internal planning work
- blocker remains for public rollout and compliance-sensitive claims

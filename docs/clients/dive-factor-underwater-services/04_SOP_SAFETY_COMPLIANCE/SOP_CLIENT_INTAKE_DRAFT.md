# SOP Draft - Client Intake

Draft only. Requires Chris review and operational/compliance review.

## Steps

1. capture client identity and callback details
2. capture market, address, marina, or landmark
3. capture asset type and concern
4. confirm urgency and requested documentation depth
5. confirm ownership or authorization
6. screen for obvious no-go conditions or out-of-scope request
7. route to quote worksheet or hold for review

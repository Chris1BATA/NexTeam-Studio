# SOP Draft - Pre-Dive Site Assessment

Draft only. Requires Chris review and operational/compliance review.

## Steps

1. verify address and access instructions
2. identify asset and shoreline context
3. assess weather, visibility expectations, current, and traffic
4. assess entanglement, contamination, and structure hazards
5. confirm permission and any marina or HOA rules
6. decide go, modified-go, or no-go

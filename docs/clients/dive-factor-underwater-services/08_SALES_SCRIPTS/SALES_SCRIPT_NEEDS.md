## Core discovery questions

Use these questions to qualify the request before quoting:

1. What asset needs to be inspected: boat, dock, lift, swim area, or other?
2. Which lake, marina, or coastal area is it in?
3. What concern triggered the request?
4. Is this routine, pre-purchase, post-storm, performance-related, or recovery-related?
5. How urgent is it?
6. What level of photo/video documentation is needed?
7. Is the site privately owned, marina-managed, or HOA-controlled?

## Script rules

- stay visual and documentation-focused unless proof supports broader claims
- do not promise compliance outcomes
- do not promise repair outcomes
- do not blend in Aquatrace brand proof unless approved
- state limitations clearly when visibility, access, or safety affects scope

# Dive Factor Underwater Services - Local SEO Research Notes

## Purpose

Capture source-backed local SEO observations from the first two priority markets.

## Hartwell takeaways

- Hartwell has strong boating scale signals from USACE and South Carolina DNR.
- Marina and rental activity suggest service pages around inspections, running gear, docks, lifts, and post-storm checks.
- Local SEO should likely prioritize:
  - Lake Hartwell underwater services
  - Lake Hartwell boat inspection
  - Lake Hartwell dock inspection
  - Lake Hartwell lift inspection

## Keowee takeaways

- Keowee supports a premium waterfront and second-home angle.
- Marina, boat-show, and waterfront-luxury signals suggest strong fit for high-trust service pages.
- Local SEO should likely prioritize:
  - Lake Keowee underwater services
  - Lake Keowee boat inspection
  - Lake Keowee dock inspection
  - Lake Keowee waterfront inspection

## Guardrails

- treat every keyword as a hypothesis until later validation
- do not create compliance-sensitive pages
- keep service-area pages internal until Chris approves site work

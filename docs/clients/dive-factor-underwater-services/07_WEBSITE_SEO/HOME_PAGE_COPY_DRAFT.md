## Intro section

Problems below the surface are easy to miss until they become expensive, inconvenient, or stressful. Dive Factor is being planned around a simple idea: help owners and waterfront partners get clearer visual information below the waterline before they are forced to guess.

## What we are planning to help with

- below-waterline checks for boats and waterfront assets
- dock and lift visual checks
- prop and running gear visual checks
- swim-area scans
- underwater photo/video documentation with written summary notes

## Who this is for

- lakefront homeowners
- second-home owners
- family boat owners
- marinas and waterfront partners

## Service-area section

Phase-one planning is focused on Lake Hartwell first and Lake Keowee second.

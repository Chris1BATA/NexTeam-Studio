## Planning-level page goals

- Home: explain the core problem, core offer, markets, and what the service is not
- Services overview: show the service buckets and route visitors to the right scope
- Below-Waterline Inspections: anchor offer page
- Boat Hull and Running Gear Inspections: boat-owner use cases
- Dock and Lift Inspections: property-owner and dock-builder use cases
- Swim-Area Hazard Checks: family and vacation-property use cases
- Lost Item Recovery: secondary page with strong scope guardrails
- Storm Damage Underwater Inspections: urgency-trigger page
- Marina and Fleet Partner Plans: B2B planning page held until partner offer is clearer
- Service Areas: local market pages only after research is sourced
- FAQ: limitations, exclusions, report expectations, and safety-minded positioning
- Contact / Request Inspection: route into the approved intake flow

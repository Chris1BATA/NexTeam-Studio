# Dive Factor Underwater Services - B2B Lead List Requirements

## Purpose

Build Google-Sheets-ready lead lists for approved future outreach planning only.

No outreach is approved from this lane yet.

## Target categories

- marinas
- boat rental companies
- boat clubs
- dock builders
- lakefront real estate agents
- vacation rental managers
- boat repair shops
- boat and yacht brokers
- HOAs and POAs
- fishing guides and charter operators
- waterfront restaurants
- campgrounds and resorts
- marine surveyors
- insurance adjusters

## Required fields

Every lead record should include:

- lead_id
- market
- county or area
- business name
- category
- website
- phone
- email if public
- address
- contact name if public
- decision-maker role if known
- service footprint
- asset footprint
- fit notes
- source URL
- source date
- source type
- last verified date
- status
- notes

## Suggested controlled values

- category: marina, rental_fleet, boat_club, dock_builder, real_estate, vacation_rental, repair_shop, broker, hoa_poa, guide_charter, restaurant, campground_resort, marine_surveyor, insurance_adjuster
- status: unresearched, researched, shortlisted, approved_for_future_outreach, hold
- market: hartwell, keowee, murray, clarks_hill_thurmond, coastal_sc, coastal_ga

## Row-quality standard

Each row should answer three questions:

1. why this lead fits Dive Factor
2. what asset base or customer base makes it relevant
3. where the data came from

## Rules

- use only source-traceable business information
- include a source URL for every row
- keep formatting Google-Sheets-ready
- do not include guessed emails
- do not contact anyone from this list without approval
- do not save private data scraped from gated systems

## Prioritization guidance

Prioritize leads that fit one or more of these conditions:

1. recurring underwater visibility needs
2. customer property under waterline risk
3. premium clientele who value documentation
4. referral leverage across many end customers
5. launch-market proximity
6. visible dock, lift, fleet, or waterfront complexity

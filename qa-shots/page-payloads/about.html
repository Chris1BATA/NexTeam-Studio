About NexTeam Studio

Built for the Trades. Not the Tech Industry.

NexTeam Studio was built because field service businesses deserve real AI — not watered-down chatbots designed for SaaS companies. The operations of a plumbing business, an HVAC shop, or an electrical contractor are fundamentally different from an office environment. The scheduling, dispatch, lead management, and job documentation challenges are unique to the trades.

So we built Nexi — an AI consultant who actually understands how field service works. She asks the right questions, builds the right plan, and deploys agents that make a real difference in your day-to-day operations. No fluff. No complexity. Just AI that works your business.

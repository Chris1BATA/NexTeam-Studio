Agent Types

AI Agents Built for Field Service Reality

Not generic chatbots. Agents that understand your operations, your trade, and the way work actually gets done in the field.

📅

Scheduling Agent

Books, reschedules, and sends reminders automatically. Your calendar runs itself — no more back-and-forth texts at 7 AM.

📡

Dispatch Agent

Assigns jobs to the right tech based on availability, skill, and location. No more phone tag to find out who's closest.

⚡

Lead Follow-Up Agent

Responds to new leads within minutes, qualifies them, and books the job — even at midnight when you're off the clock.

📝

Job Documentation Agent

Techs log job notes, parts used, and close-out details in seconds. Invoices have clean data and nothing falls through the cracks.

🗺️

Route Optimization Agent

Plans daily routes to minimize drive time and maximize jobs per day. Less windshield time, more billable hours.

💬

Customer Communication Agent

Sends arrival windows, job updates, and follow-ups automatically. Customers stay informed without you lifting a finger.

About NexTeam Studio

Built for the Trades. Not the Tech Industry.

NexTeam Studio was built because field service businesses deserve real AI — not watered-down chatbots designed for SaaS companies. The operations of a plumbing business, an HVAC shop, or an electrical contractor are fundamentally different from an office environment. So we built Nexi — an AI consultant who actually understands how field service works. She asks the right questions, builds the right plan, and deploys agents that make a real difference in your day-to-day operations. No fluff. No complexity. Just AI that works your business.

Service Menu

What this lane covers.

Below-Waterline Inspection

Visual review of hull-adjacent areas, visible fouling, running gear concerns, and other underwater observations supported by photos or video.

Dock and Lift Visual Inspection

Underwater visibility around dock structures, lift contact zones, and surrounding areas where owners want a clearer picture.

Prop / Running Gear Visual Check

Useful when a boat owner wants a visual check for obvious wrap, fouling, or visible damage signals below the surface.

Swim Area Hazard Scan

A practical underwater scan around recreational swim zones, camp waterfronts, and managed access areas.

Lost Item Recovery

Targeted recovery support when location conditions, visibility, and scope are suitable for a focused search.

Marina Partner Support

Documentation-oriented underwater support for marinas, dock builders, rental fleets, and waterfront operators.

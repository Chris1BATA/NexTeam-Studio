About Dive Factor

Documentation-Led Underwater Support

Dive Factor Underwater Services is positioned as a professional underwater service focused on visual inspection, below-waterline documentation, and practical condition reporting for boats, docks, lifts, marinas, lake homeowners, and waterfront properties.

Led by Chris Sears, the brand keeps the premium, adventure-forward Dive Factor look while shifting the business focus away from scuba training and guided expeditions toward photo/video documentation, visual checks, and underwater support for real assets and real property concerns.

The phase-one offer direction is inspection-first: help owners and partners see what is happening below the waterline before they guess, schedule repairs, or escalate the next step.
